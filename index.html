Our Story

At Heavy Heart Vintage, the passion for antique collecting runs deep, a tradition passed down through generations of the Gilland family. What began with a love for preserving history through antiques evolved into a passion for vintage clothing. For Parker Gilland, the excitement is not just in finding forgotten pieces but in giving them a second life—connecting the past with the present and sharing those stories with others. Parker spends over 300 days each year traveling the country, sourcing and collecting unique vintage clothing. Each journey is an adventure, digging through forgotten corners to find treasures with timeless character. 

 

While not every piece makes it to the store, those that do are carefully selected for their craftsmanship, history, and the unique stories they carry. What sets Heavy Heart Vintage apart is its ability to help customers truly understand the value behind each piece. 

 

Whether it's explaining the difference between an $80 pair of Levi’s and an $800 pair, or exploring the distinction between a $120 leather jacket and a $650 one, Parker makes it his mission to educate and guide. It's about more than just selling clothing—it's about sharing the knowledge of what makes these pieces special and helping others appreciate the artistry behind them.
